Frequently Asked Questions

What is DepChip?

DepChip is a product development partner that helps you go from idea to launch with speed. We build web and mobile apps, design clean user experiences, and set up the tech that keeps your business running smoothly. You focus on the vision; we handle the build.

Who can benefit from DepChip’s services?

Our services are built for startups, growing businesses, and enterprises that want to launch or scale their digital products quickly. Whether you need a full tech team or just expert support, we help you move faster and build smarter.

How does DepChip ensure fast and reliable delivery?

We use lean, agile processes and modern tools to build efficiently without cutting corners. Every project goes through clear milestones i.e from planning and design to deployment, ensuring speed, transparency, and top quality at every step.

Can DepChip help after my product is launched?

Yes, absolutely. We offer post-launch support, feature updates, cloud management, and performance monitoring to keep your product stable and ready to grow. Think of us as your long-term tech partner, not just a development agency.
